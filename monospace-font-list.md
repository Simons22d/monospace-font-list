## [TT2020](https://ctrlcctrlv.github.io/TT2020/docs/)

-   anchorName: tt2020

-   notes:

        In response to a few rather obvious examples of poor typewriter
        font realism in recent movies, Fredrick Brennan created TT2020.
        
        Styled after early 20th-century typewriter faces, TT2020
        includes a clean variant as well as a variety of distressed
        variants.  Each variant comes with nine slightly different
        variants of each character, from which one is pseudo-randomly
        chosen when rendered, adding a degree of realism not likely
        hithertofore seen in typewriter fonts.
        
        Another example of a typeface that pseudo-randomly chooses from
        a number of variants of each character is Neue, the corporate
        font used by The New School <https://www.newschool.edu/>.
        
        TT2020 includes upright and italic variants.
